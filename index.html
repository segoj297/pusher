Save the Philippines from Drug Pushers!

BRING HOME THE PHILIPPINE WAR ON DRUGS
2 PLAYERS • ALL AGES

Drug addicts, drug dealers, and drug PUSHERS live everywhere in the Philippines' poor neighborhoods. Care and rehabilitation for them is too expensive. There's only one thing to do: KILL THEM ALL!

PUSHER is a competitive tabletop game that puts you in the role of an anti-drug squad! Eliminate the most PUSHERS from the streets by shooting or imprisoning them before your ammo runs out!
